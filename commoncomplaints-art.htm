Common Complaints, 2016, created in the UK. In this video performance I collaborated with Claire McCue who composed a score to accompany the video. This score was recored live on the 27 April 2016 at the Assembly Hall in Glasgow with the New Music Expedition Ensemble, led by Richard Greer and conducted by Jessica Cottis.

 

Credits: Alasdair Garrett - Flute, Jenny Stephenson - Clarinet, Chris McShane - Bassoon, Glynn Forrest - Percussion, Liam Eoin Lynch - Violin, Elaine Koene - Viola, Andrew Huggan - Cello, Ed Lucas - Double Bass. Video of live performance produced by Alex Horowitz, Douglas Whates, sound engineer.
